Farkle

Objective:

Be the player with the highest score over 10,000. You get one more chance if the other player reaches 10,000 first

Scoring:

Single 1 = 100       Single 5 = 50

Three 1's = 1000       Three 2's = 200

Three 3's = 300       Three 4's = 400

Three 5's = 500       Three 6's = 600

Four of a kind = 1000       Five of a kind = 2000

Six of a kind = 3,000      Straight = 1,500

Three pairs = 1,500       Two triplets = 2,500

Controls:

Press ROLL to roll the dice

Click the dice you would like to keep (you must click at least 1)

If you wan't to bank your cumulative score press BANK
